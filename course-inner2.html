Course Overview

LEE University's introduction to the intellectual enterprises of computer science and the art of programming for majors and non-majors alike, with or without prior programming experience. An entry-level course taught by Ema Irnik. HTML (the Hypertext Markup Language) and CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) are two of the core technologies for building Web pages. HTML provides the structure of the page, CSS the (visual and aural) layout, for a variety of devices. Also students who earn a satisfactory score on 9 problem sets (i.e., programming assignments) and a final project are eligible for a certificate.
